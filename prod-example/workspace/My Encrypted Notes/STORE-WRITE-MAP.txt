==========================================

  workspace/My Encrypted Notes/notes/demo-note.txt

  .upriv/vaults/my-encrypted-notes/store/data/<hash>/….chunk.enc
  .upriv/vaults/my-encrypted-notes/store/index/root.idx.enc
  .upriv/vaults/my-encrypted-notes/persistence.json  → last_store_write_at

  .upriv/vaults/my-encrypted-notes/archive/My Encrypted Notes.7z

  .upriv/state.json          → session: open
